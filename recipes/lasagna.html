Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, this richly layered dish swimming in sumptuous tomato sauce, made its debut in Naples, Italy, during the Middle Ages.

In this occasion, I will be teaching you how to make an original made-from-scratch lasagna. We will be making the tomato and bechamel sauce, and the pasta dough, everything from SCRATCH!

Ingredients

  • 600 g veal meat
  • 300 g bacon
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 celery stick
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 800 g peeled tomatoes
  • 50 ml milk
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Grounded black pepper to taste

Pasta

  • 200g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • a pinch of salt

Bechamel sauce

  • 80 g butter
  • 80 g flour
  • 750 ml milk
  • Nutmeg to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Grounded black pepper to taste
  • 200 g parmesan cheese

Step by step

Meat sauce (Ragu)

  1. Cut the bacon in brunoise.
  2. Cook the bacon in medium heat.
  3. Cut the vegetables in brunoise as well.
  4. Add a dash of olive oil, to the pan where we are cooking the bacon.
  5. Add the vegetables
  6. Add a pinch of salt
  7. Cook the onion, th carrots and the celery over lower heat, until they are soft.
  8. Add the veal meat
  9. Cook over high heat and stir it well
  10. Once the meat changes the color, add a glass of red wine
  11. Cook until the alcohol has evaporated
  12. Add the peeled tomatoes
  13. Once the sauce starts to boil, change to lower heat. Let it cook for 2 hours (add water if needed)
  14. Add a pinch of grounded black pepper
  15. Add milk to rectify acidity.
  16. The sauce is ready!!

Homemade dough

  1. On a bowl, sift the flour.
  2. Add a pich of salt.
  3. Add 2 eggs.
  4. Add a dash of olive oil.
  5. Stir it well.
  6. Do not knead the dough too much, you just need to integrate te ingredients.
  7. Let it rest for at least 20 minutes.
  8. Roll ot the dough with a rolling pin.
  9. The ideal width of the dough is when it starts to become transparent.
  10. Cut the dough in layers of 10x12 cm. You will obtain at least 17 layers.
  11. Cook them well in salty water, don't add them all at once!!
  12. ONce cooked, place them on a cloth to dry them.

Bechamel sauce

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Add the flour.
  3. Stir them well and gently for about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the cold milk and stir it at high heat, gently.
  5. You have to stir all the time, gently, until it's cooked.
  6. Once it starts boiling, cook it for 2-3 more minutes and remove from the fire.
  7. Add salt and pepper.
  8. To prevent crust from forming you can cover it with plastic wrap.

FINALLY!!

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C or 360C.
  2. Grease the mold with butter.
  3. Cover the bottom with a layer of bechamel sauce.
  4. Add pasta layers.
  5. Add a layer of meat sauce (Ragu).
  6. Add bechamel sauce.
  7. Add grated parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the steps, add pasta layers, ragu, bechamel and parmesan cheese, until you reach the top.
  9. Cover the last layer with bechamel and parmesan.
  10. Bake it for 45 minutes.
  11. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving.
  12. Enjoy!!!
