Pancakes

Description

The tea time has arrived,and you don't have enough money to go buy something to eat, and you don't want to go out either, so what can you do?

Follow me, I'll teach you...the old reliable.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1-2 tbsp of oil
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • A dash of vanilla essence
  • A pinch of salt

Step by step

  1. Grab your blender, add the milk, the egg, the oil, the sugar, the essence, the salt, and the flour.
  2. Mix it.
  3. Grease your pan with butter. Heat it at low heat.
  4. Pour the mix little by little to your hot pan, until you have created a layer of the pancake mix. Not to thick, not to thin.
  5. Cook both sides 2-3 min each. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  6. Place them on a plate

Serving!

When serving the pancakes you have some options you can add for more flavor. Some options are the following:

  • Top with fresh fruits. Add a healthy twist with sliced bananas, berries or apple slices.
  • Maple syrup and butter.
  • Nut butter and Honey. For a protein/packed topping.
